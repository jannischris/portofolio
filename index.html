About

I am a telecommunications and business operations professional specializing in strategic execution, performance optimization, and large-scale technical project management. With a Bachelor’s degree in Informatics, Computer & Telecommunications Engineering and an MBA in progress, I combine strong technical expertise with structured business acumen.

Currently serving as an FTTx Rollout Manager in Germany, I lead multi-million-euro fiber network deployment projects, coordinating cross-functional teams and ensuring operational efficiency, financial control, and high-quality delivery standards. My experience spans budget management (P&L, forecasting), supplier negotiations, workforce planning, and the development of executive-level reporting dashboards using Power BI and Smartsheet.

My professional approach is defined by leadership, analytical decision-making, and the ability to perform under pressure. I focus on building scalable operational structures, aligning teams with strategic objectives, and delivering measurable business impact through disciplined execution.
